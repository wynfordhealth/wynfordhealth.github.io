Chiropractic

Chiropractors specialize in treating dysfunctions in your muscles, soft tissues, joints, bones, and the nervous system. When you come in for chiropractic treatments you will receive a hands-on approach to healthcare that is drug free, highly effective and safe. Chiropractors assess, diagnose, treat and provide prevention strategies that will reduce your pain, restore your function and eliminate the chance of injury recurrences. Based on the assessment findings, your chiropractor will develop a customized treatment plan that will include chiropractic adjustments, muscle release techniques, manual therapies, acupuncture, physical therapies, performance taping and other specialized hands-on techniques in order to resolve your aches, pains and injuries.

Massage Therapy

Wynford Health Clinic provides well-trained and knowledgeable registered massage therapists to assist the patient on the road to recovery. They use a variety of hands on techniques to manipulate the soft tissues in your body (muscles, connective tissues, joints, tendons and ligaments) in order to provide therapeutic relief, decrease your pain and optimize your health.

Acupuncture

Acupuncture has been practiced in China for several thousand years. This form of therapy is used to treat headaches, muscle pain, back pain, and a large variety of other musculoskeletal conditions. It is especially safe and effective when used in conjunction with the other forms of treatment offered at the clinic.

Osteopathy

Osteopathy is a drug-free, non-invasive manual therapy that aims to improve health across all body systems by manipulating and strengthening the musculoskeletal framework. An osteopathic physician will focus on the joints, muscles, and spine. Treatment aims to positively affect the body’s nervous, circulatory, and lymphatic systems.

Physiotherapy

Physiotherapy is the therapeutic application of forces and substances that induce a physiological response and allow the body’s natural processes to return to a more normal state of health. Many physiotherapeutic modalities exist to assist patients in accelerating their recovery. These treatments aim to control pain and help to promote tissue healing by reducing the inflammation process. According to the treatment plan the patient may receive a variety of modalities including Laser, Ultrasound, Heat, Ice, TENS and IFC.

Physical Rehabilitation

Physical Rehabilitation is an integral part of the patient's recovery. It is the development of exercise protocols and processes that assist the body is returning to a more normal state of health. Whether you are young, old, male, female, big or small, if you are experiencing some form of disease then you will benefit from exercises. The patient's level of fitness is evaluated according to a series of standardized flexibility, strength, endurance, and cardiovascular tests. From the initial test results, each patient receives their own customized active program, which is designed and supervised by our team of professionals. The program consists of stretching, resistance exercises, and cardiovascular conditioning. Our clinic provides all necessary physical rehabilitation equipment.

Spinal Decompression Therapy (SDT)

Spinal Decompression therapy (SDT) is a computerized stretching of the spine that creates space between the vertebrae and relieves pressure on the bones, nerves, and joints. This painless and non-surgical treatment gently stretches the spine back to its proper position and relieves pressures and pain.

Learning to cope with mental struggles could greatly improve your quality of life. Our specialists provide individualized psychological assessment and treatment to adults, children, and adolescents for a variety of problems.

Pedorthic Footcare

Every person is unique: so are their feet. In an ideal world, all shoes would be made individually taking into account your physique, posture, and even personality. Yet, there is a way. Our pedorthists provide comprehensive assessments of the biomechanics of your feet and lower limbs. They design and make custom foot orthotics for you and recommend or modifying footwear. Your custom foot orthotics will be as unique as you are.
