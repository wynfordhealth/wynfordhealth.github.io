About Wynford Health Clinic

We are a multidisciplinary care facility. We serve patients suffering from physical, functional and psychological dysfunctions. Our professional team at Wynford Health Clinic strives to provide you with the tools necessary for a proper and safe recovery. We offer massage therapy, chiropractic treatment, acupuncture, osteopathy, psychotherapy, physiotherapy, and physical therapy. Our highly-trained professionals are committed to providing our patients with the highest standards of competency and care.

Services

Chiropractic

Chiropractors specialize in treating dysfunctions in your muscles, soft tissues, joints, bones, and nerves.

Massage Therapy

Massage therapy can help to relieve stress and anxiety, relax muscles, relieve tension, headaches, enhance and maintain good health.

Medical Acupuncture

Acupuncture can help to treat headaches, muscle pain, back pain, and many other musculoskeletal conditions.

Osteopathy

Osteopathy is a drug-free, non-invasive manual therapy that improves health across all body systems.

Physiotherapy

Physiotherapy is the therapeutic application of forces and substances that induce normalizing of the body’s natural processes.

Physical Rehabilitation

Physical Rehabilitation is an integral part of the patient's recovery and returning to a more normal state of health.

Spinal Decompression Therapy

Spinal Decompression Therapy (SDT) is a computerized stretching of the spine to increase space between the vertebrae.

Psychotherapy

Your mental health is important. If you are struggling with behavioural, emotional or mental health issues, we are here to help.

Pedorthic Footcare

Our pedorthists examine the biomechanics of your feet and lower limbs. Your custom foot orthotics will be unique.
